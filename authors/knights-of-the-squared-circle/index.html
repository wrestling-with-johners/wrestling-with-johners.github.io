    Knights Of The Squared Circle

    Podcast

    Knights of the Squared Circle is a weekly radio show and podcast hosted by Ryan and Ariena Knight featuring interviews with Canada’s most talented independent wrestlers and a variety of wrestling figures. Available live every Sunday at 12 pm est on Coyote 103 and our website www.knightsofthesc.com with replays on Facebook, and Spotify.

    Knights of the Squared Circle also host VIP sessions before live events featuring tapings of Knights with talent working that event, Q&A for the fans, giveaways and more!

    Ryan also appears with multiple promotions conducting pre/post match interviews for social media and event tapings, as well as different promos and segments for shows.
